What We Prepare

The Full Range

Everything below is prepared on the premises. Ask in-store about specific cuts, quantities for a freezer order, or anything not listed here — if it's a traditional butcher's item, there's a good chance we can sort it for you.

Fresh Beef

Joints, steaks, mince and diced beef, cut fresh to order — from a Sunday roast to a quick midweek stir-fry.

Fresh Lamb

Leg, shoulder, loin and chops alongside neck, liver and kidneys for those who like to cook the traditional way.

Fresh Pork

Leg, shoulder and chops, plus belly, hock and ribs — good honest cuts for roasting, slow-cooking or the grill.

Poultry

Whole chickens and turkey, breasts, legs and drumsticks — everyday poultry, always fresh.

Homemade Sausages & Burgers

Made right here on the premises — not bought in — alongside plain and smoked bacon, gammon and dry cure.

Seasonal Game

Fresh local pheasant and other game in season — keep an eye on our window, or ask us what's currently in.

Fresh Local Pheasant, In Season
