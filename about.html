Our Story

A genuine family butcher, run the traditional way

Steve Quigley & Sons is exactly what the sign says — a traditional family butcher's shop on Cubbington Road in Lillington, run by the Quigley family for the local community.

Who We Are

A family shop, not a chain

Steve Quigley & Sons is an independent, family-run butcher's shop — the kind of place where the person cutting your meat is the same person whose name is above the door. There's no head office, no corporate menu of standardised cuts; just the Quigley family, serving Lillington and the wider Leamington Spa area the way a proper high-street butcher always has.

Our sign tells you most of what you need to know before you've even stepped inside: "Traditional Family Butchers," "Finest Quality Meat," and suppliers of fresh beef, lamb, pork and poultry. It's a straightforward promise, and it's one we keep every day the shop is open — right down to the prices, which we still chalk up by hand on the boards in-store rather than print.

How We Work

Proper butchery, honest service

Three things you can rely on every time you visit, in-store or by phone.

Fresh, quality-first meat

We're suppliers of the finest quality fresh beef, lamb, pork and poultry — the same standard our shopfront has always promised, and the same standard we hold ourselves to every day.

Homemade, not bought in

Our sausages and burgers are made on the premises, not shipped in from a supplier — it's a genuine point of pride, and it's why customers keep coming back for them specifically.

A shop that knows its regulars

As a small family business, we get to know the people we serve — what they usually order, what they're cooking for, and how they like it cut. That's something a supermarket counter can't offer.
